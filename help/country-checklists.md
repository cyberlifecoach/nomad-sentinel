# Country Security Checklists

Pre-built security and safety checklists for 18 countries covering border rules, local scams, SIM card laws, and cyber risk levels. Use these to prepare before you arrive, not after.

---

## Covered countries

| Region | Countries |
|---|---|
| North America | USA, Canada, Mexico |
| Central America | Costa Rica, Panama |
| Europe | UK, Portugal, Spain, Germany, France, Greece, Denmark |
| Caucasus & Middle East | Georgia, Turkey |
| Asia | Thailand, Vietnam, Cambodia, South Korea |

---

## How to use

1. Click **Country Guide** in the sidebar.
2. Select a country from the list.
3. Review the checklist categories for that country.
4. Check off items as you complete your pre-travel preparation.

---

## Checklist categories

Each country checklist covers:

### Border & Entry
Visa requirements, entry restrictions, customs rules, and items that may be flagged at the border including encrypted devices, VPNs, and certain medications.

### Cyber Risk
Local internet censorship level, VPN legality, public Wi-Fi risks, and known surveillance practices.

### SIM & Communications
Local SIM card registration laws, recommended carriers, whether registration requires biometrics or passport scanning.

### Local Scams
Common scams targeting travelers in that country — transport scams, accommodation fraud, ATM skimming hotspots.

### Emergency Contacts
Local emergency numbers, embassy contact information, and recommended local resources.

## User notes

Each checklist item has a notes field where you can add your own observations. Your notes are saved per profile and preserved when the app is updated.

---

## Updating country data

Country checklists are bundled with the app and updated with each Nomad Sentinel release. To get the latest checklists, download the newest release from GitHub.

Your custom notes and overrides are preserved across updates.
