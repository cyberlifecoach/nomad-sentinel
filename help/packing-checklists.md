# Packing Checklists

Load pre-built packing templates by trip type or build your own custom lists. Check items off as you pack, export your list, and save custom lists for future trips.

---

## Pre-built templates

Nomad Sentinel includes starter templates for the most common trip types:

| Template | Best for |
|---|---|
| City Break | Short urban trips, business travel |
| Beach & Island | Warm weather, water activities |
| Workation | Extended remote work trips |
| High-Risk Destination | Journalists, activists, high-surveillance regions |
| Backpacker | Long-term travel, minimal gear |

---

## Loading a template

1. Click **Packing Lists** in the sidebar.
2. Click **New List**.
3. Select a template from the grid.
4. Give your list a name — for example, "Lisbon June 2026".
5. Click **Create**. The template items are loaded and ready to edit.

---

## Customizing a list

- Click **Add Item** to add a new item to any category.
- Click the checkbox next to an item to mark it as packed.
- Click **Delete** on any item to remove it from the list.
- Built-in template items can be checked off or deleted but the original templates are never modified.

---

## Managing multiple lists

You can create as many lists as you need. All lists are shown in the Packing Lists sidebar. Click any list to open it.

To delete a list, open it and click **Delete List** at the bottom of the page. This is permanent and cannot be undone.

---

## Exporting a list

1. Open the list you want to export.
2. Click **Export CSV**.
3. The file is saved to your app data directory. The path is shown in the confirmation dialog.

---

## Important notes

> **Lists are stored per device.** Packing lists are not included in the encrypted backup in v1. Export to CSV if you need a portable copy.

> **The Back button** returns you to the template picker, not an empty state. If you want to start fresh, click **New List**.
