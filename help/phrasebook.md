# Phrasebook

Access essential phrases in 20+ languages organized by scenario. All phrases are stored locally and available offline. No internet connection required.

---

## How to use

1. Click **Phrasebook** in the sidebar.
2. Select a language from the list.
3. Browse phrases by scenario, or use the search bar to find a specific phrase.

---

## Scenarios

Phrases are organized into the following scenarios:

| Scenario | Examples |
|---|---|
| Greetings | Hello, goodbye, please, thank you |
| Emergency | Call the police, I need a doctor, I need help |
| Transport | Where is the airport, how much does this cost, stop here |
| Accommodation | I have a reservation, where is the exit, do you have Wi-Fi |
| Food & Drink | I am vegetarian, no spice, the bill please |
| Shopping | How much does this cost, do you accept cards, too expensive |
| Communications | Where is the nearest pharmacy, I need to charge my phone |
| Border & Immigration | I am a tourist, here is my passport, I do not understand |

---

## Adding your own phrases

You can add custom phrases to any language pack.

1. Open the language you want to add to.
2. Click **Add Phrase**.
3. Select a scenario, enter the phrase in the local language, and add an English translation.
4. Click **Save**.

Your custom phrases appear alongside the built-in phrases in the relevant scenario.

---

## Deleting phrases

You can delete phrases you have added yourself. Built-in phrases cannot be deleted — this ensures a clean update path when new phrase packs are released.

1. Find the phrase you want to delete.
2. Click **Delete** next to it.
3. Confirm when prompted. Deletion is permanent.

---

## Searching phrases

Use the search bar at the top of the phrasebook to filter by keyword across all scenarios in the selected language. Search matches on both the local phrase and the English translation.

---

## Important notes

> **Phrases are stored as plain text.** The phrasebook is not encrypted — it is not intended for sensitive information. Use the Encrypted Journal for private notes.

> **Built-in phrases are read-only.** You can add and delete your own phrases but the built-in content is never modified. This protects your data when the app updates.

> **Audio pronunciation is not available in v1.** Pronunciation guides are planned for a future release.
