# Secure Setup Wizard

A step-by-step guide to hardening your devices before you travel. The wizard walks you through VPN selection, browser hardening, DNS configuration, and encrypted sync options.

The setup wizard is advisory only — it does not make any changes to your system. All steps are recommendations you apply yourself.

---

## Who this is for

- Travelers heading to high-risk or high-surveillance destinations
- Anyone connecting to public Wi-Fi in airports, hotels, or cafés
- Journalists and remote workers who need to secure their devices quickly

---

## How it works

1. Click **Setup Wizard** in the sidebar.
2. Work through each category in order, or jump to the section most relevant to your situation.
3. Check off each item as you complete it. Your progress is saved per profile.

---

## Categories

### VPN
Recommendations for trusted VPN providers with no-log policies and strong encryption. Includes direct links to Mullvad, IVPN, and ProtonVPN.

> **Tip:** Set your VPN to connect automatically on untrusted networks. Most providers support a kill switch that blocks traffic if the VPN drops.

### Browser hardening
Step-by-step settings for Firefox and Chromium-based browsers including:
- Container tabs to isolate browsing sessions
- WebRTC leak prevention
- Cookie and site data settings
- Canvas fingerprinting protection
- Recommended about:config tweaks for Firefox
- Privacy-focused search engine setup

### DNS over HTTPS
Configure your browser or OS to use encrypted DNS to prevent ISP-level snooping on your lookups.

### Encrypted sync
Options for syncing files and notes across devices without exposing them to cloud providers.

---

## Saving your progress

Each checklist item is saved individually as you check it off. Progress is stored per profile — switching profiles will show that profile's own progress.

---

## Resetting the wizard

To reset your progress and start fresh, go to **Settings** and select **Reset Setup Wizard** for the active profile.
